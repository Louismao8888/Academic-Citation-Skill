# Citation Format Templates

Reference for Stage 3 of the paper-references skill.

---

## APA 7th Edition

**Sort order:** Alphabetical by first author last name.

### Journal article
`Last, F. M., & Last, F. M. (Year). Title of article. *Journal Name*, *Volume*(Issue), Page-Page. https://doi.org/xxxxx`

**Example:**
`Vaswani, A., Shazeer, N., Parmar, N., Uszkoreit, J., Jones, L., Gomez, A. N., Kaiser, L., & Polosukhin, I. (2017). Attention is all you need. *Advances in Neural Information Processing Systems*, *30*, 5998-6008.`

**Key rules:**
- Use "&" between final two authors (not "and")
- Up to 20 authors; for 21+ list first 19, "...", then last author
- DOI as URL: `https://doi.org/xxxxx`
- Sentence case for article titles; title case for journal names
- White papers/reports: add `[White paper]` or `[Report]` after title

### Book
`Last, F. M. (Year). *Title of book* (Xth ed.). Publisher.`

### Conference paper
`Last, F. M. (Year). Title of paper. In *Proceedings of Conference Name* (pp. Page-Page). Publisher.`

## MLA 9th Edition

**Sort order:** Alphabetical by first author last name.

### Journal article
`Last, First, and First Last. "Title of Article." *Journal Name*, vol. X, no. Y, Year, pp. Page-Page. DOI.`

**Key rules:** Title case for all titles. "et al." for 3+ authors.

---

## Chicago 17th (Notes-Bibliography)

### Journal article
`Last, First. "Title of Article." *Journal Name* Vol, no. Issue (Year): Page-Page. https://doi.org/xxxxx.`

### Book
`Last, First. *Title of Book*. City: Publisher, Year.`

---

## Vancouver

**Sort order:** Numbered in order of first appearance in text.

### Journal article
`Last FM, Last FM. Title of article. J Abbrev. Year;Vol(Issue):Page-Page.`

**Key rules:** No spaces in initials. Up to 6 authors, then "et al."

---

## IEEE

**Sort order:** Numbered in order of appearance. In-text: [1].

### Journal article
`[1] F. M. Last and F. M. Last, "Title," *Journal Abbrev.*, vol. X, no. Y, pp. ZZ-ZZ, Year, doi: xxxxx.`

### Conference paper
`[2] F. M. Last, "Title," in *Proc. Conference (ABBREV)*, City, Year, pp. ZZ-ZZ.`

---

## GB/T 7714-2015 (Chinese National Standard)

**Sort order:** Numbered in order of appearance.

### Chinese journal article
`[序号] 作者. 文章题目[J]. 刊名, 年份, 卷(期): 起止页码. DOI.`

### English article
`[序号] LAST F, LAST F. Title[J]. Journal Name, Year, Vol(Issue): Page-Page. DOI.`

**Type codes:** [J]=期刊 [M]=专著 [C]=会议论文集 [D]=学位论文 [R]=报告 [S]=标准 [EB/OL]=网络资源

## Matching a User-Supplied Format

Analyze the example and extract:
1. Author format (First Last / Last, F. M. / ALL CAPS?)
2. Multiple-author separator and "et al." cutoff
3. Year position (after authors / at end / in parentheses)
4. Title style (quoted / italics / title case / sentence case)
5. Journal name (full / abbreviated / italicized)
6. Volume/issue/page format
7. DOI format and presence
8. Trailing punctuation

Apply extracted rules uniformly to all entries.
